Follow-Up Care Reimbursement — Member Flow
Follow-up care billing generates additional revenue on every clinically indicated visit, while giving members the ability to use their insurance exactly where they would expect it to apply — reinforcing Neko as a medical service, not a wellness product.
01
Sign-Up & Intake Form
During the intake form, members are invited to add their insurance details. We explain that insurance is not used for the scan itself, but may be relevant if a follow-up visit is recommended. Providing insurance details at this stage is optional — members can choose to skip it or add details later.
Optional at sign-up Can be added later
02
Neko Scan & Consultation
Full-body scan and physician consultation. The scan is $499 for all members — no insurance is involved at this stage. Approximately 30% of scans generate a finding that warrants a follow-up visit (BP 10%, EKG 9%, dermoscopy 8%, echo 6%, ABPI 1%).
$499 for all members ~30% proceed to follow-up

05
For members choosing Option B — insurance
Visit
Follow-Up at Neko
Member attends follow-up at Neko. Physician conducts the indicated procedure (e.g. 12-lead EKG, echo, dermoscopy, BP review). No payment is made to Neko at the point of visit.
Billing
Bridge Bills Insurer & Pays Out Neko
Bridge submits the E/M visit and applicable CPT procedure codes to the member's insurer, handles claims and revenue cycle management, and passes reimbursement to Neko. Average ~$111 per visit (blended 50/50 MD/NP model, subsequent visit rates).
